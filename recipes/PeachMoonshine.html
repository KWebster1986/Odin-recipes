The still at Ozark Distillery

Summer afternoons around the camper or at the pool occasionally calls for a relaxing drink. One of my favorite adult beverages happens to be moonshine and Ozark Distillery moonshine offers a lot of options!! On this post I'll be sharing recipes I make with their peach flavor! If you are ever in the Lake of The Ozarks, Missouri, take an afternnon and visit the distillery. They offer tastings and a full resturant. You can also schedule a tour!! Its a neat experiance and a great way to spend an afternoon in the AC.

Peachy Keen

I stole this picture from pinterest

Another super easy summer drink I love is peach tea and peach moonshine. This one is a little less deliberate in exicution so please bear with me.
  1. Make or buy your favorite peach ice tea and put in into a pitch just a little too big. We need room!!
  2. Grab you peach moonshine and give it a pour!! I tend to be a little heavy handed here but that's not always a bad thing
  3. Cut up a fresh peach and garnish the top of the pitcher and any glasses you plan to use
  4. Pour your lovely tea into glasses and serve to guests!
